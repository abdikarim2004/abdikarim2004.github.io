About Mosque Medina

Welcome to Mosque Medina! We are a vibrant Islamic community center located in Varissuo, Turku. Our mission is to serve the needs of our members and build a strong and supportive Muslim community.

At Mosque Medina, we are dedicated to preserving our Islamic identity and promoting a comprehensive way of life based on the teachings of the Holy Quran and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad PBUH. We believe that by offering a welcoming mosque center, we can help our community members connect with one another, grow in faith, and live meaningful and fulfilling lives.

Whether you are looking for a place to worship, attend lectures or socialize with other members of the Muslim community, Mosque Medina has something for you. We offer regular prayer services, as well as a variety of events throughout the year. Our community is made up of diverse individuals from many different ethnicities and backgrounds, but we are all united by islam.

If you are new to the area or new to Islam, we invite you to visit Mosque Medina and discover the beauty of our center. We are a warm and welcoming community, and we look forward to getting to know you!
