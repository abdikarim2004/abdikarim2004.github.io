welcome to the Mosque Medina

Mosque Medina is a vibrant community center that serves the needs of its members. With a commitment to preserving an Islamic identity and promoting a comprehensive way of life based on the teachings of the Holy Quran and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad PBUH, it is dedicated to building a strong and supportive Muslim community. Whether for religious observances, social events, or educational programs, it serves as a cornerstone for its members and a beacon for the wider community.

Thank you for visiting the Mosque Medina website. It is located in Varissuo, Turku, and serves as both a mosque and a community center for Muslims. We invite you to discover the beauty of our center and meet our diverse and friendly community. Hope to see you soon!
